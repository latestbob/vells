SOURCING

We source the best products and services with an out- standing balance of the quality of products and services for clients at the best cost.

We achieve this by partnering with the right suppliers and providing quality services and products at the best price. We create a seamless pipeline to procure these products and bring them to our clients, keeping the process func- tioning effectively and efficiently.

TRANSPORTATION & LOGISTICS

We plan the logistics by obtaining and distributing mate- rials and products of the right qualities to our clients. Our logistics process considers the science of planning to manage and implement procedures for the most efficient and effective storage and transportation of goods and services.

Furthermore, efficient transportation is essential to our distribution service. Therefore, we analyse the best deliv- ery route, the most cost-effective packaging, safest routes of goods that ensure the best value at the least cost.

SUPPLY OF GENERAL GOODS

We sign and execute general contracts to supply and distribute finished goods, services, materials, equipment, etc., to clients. We supply all kinds of products to our clients on demand. We aim to create a means through which our clients can buy or get supplies of all kinds of products they need from the comfort of their offices by simply contacting Vells Resourcery.

We aim to take away the stress of liaising with many sup- pliers to deliver items. Instead, we provide convenience to all clients.

MEDICAL CONSUMABLES & EQUIPMENT

Working with our global strategic partners, we source and de- liver door–to–door medical equipment and consumables.

Our competence and experience include medical consum- ables, Operation theatre equipment, all classes (i.e. I, II and III) of medical equipment, non–medical equipment, furniture etc.
