Welcome to VELLS Resourcery Limited

Vells Resourcery Limited is an independent commodity trading company. We operate globally across various value chains, including sourcing, transporting, storage, and financing medical consumables and equip- ment, among other sectors.

We are also involved in solid minerals mining, oil & gas and alternative energy, Smart technologies, and general goods and services supply chain.
